Choose UserName and Password Wisely !!!

User Name :: UserName must be
permutation of characters from 'a-z / A-Z' ,
digits from '0-9' and '@' or '_'
starting from an alphabet
Ex. John_123,etc. Its length
must not exceed
15 characters.

NOTE -"NO OTHER CHARACTERS APART FROM THESE SHOULD BE USED".

Password :: Password span must be atleast of 6 characters but must not exceed 15 characters.
It can be a combination of any type of characters , numerals or special symbols.
